Artsy Fartsy

I began drawing at 12 years old, but didn't really love it until I was about 14. My mom had always been one of my biggest art influences. I both admired and envied how talented she was and always wondered why I couldn't draw anything more than a stick figure. It was from that moment forward that I decided I would try and better myself. Till this day, drawing or sketching is something that provides me with a major peace of mind. I'm no Picasso or anything, but I think that when it comes to art, that honestly doesn't mean anything, as art can be found in anything and anyone. It's everywhere.
