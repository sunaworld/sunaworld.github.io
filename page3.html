Take a walk through my first semester journey as a news reporter at the New School Free press!

NSFP ARTICLE 1

This is the first story that I got published at the paper. Techincally it's the second story that I wrote, but deadline complications caused some glitches. However, this piece was centered around data reporting for the school's rank drop and was incredibly fun to write!

NSFP ARTICLE 2

This is my first article, which was published as my second. This story meant a lot to me because it was the first time I got to try our professional journalism. My editor put a lot of faith in me to handle this as my first story, and really helped me get accustomed to news writing. I learned a lot during those few weeks and recieved so much praise and support, it was such a wholesome experience.

NSFP ARTICLE 3

This news story is my third and it was a really big one. It was about how we have professors living in student residence halls. This experience was another great one, as I was able to not only interview a professor that lives in the dorm and find out her experience, but I was invited to do the interview in her apartmenet. I was even asked to come back a second time to be accompanied by a photographer. It was all super cool!

NSFP ARTICLE 4

This is my most recently published article, surrounding international students and the difficulties they faced/are facing within transitioning to a school in a new city and navigating through the help of ISSS. I am incredibly proud of this article for two reasons. 1. I got to interview so many amazing people, and I am so proud of the way I conducted them given that the topic was pretty sensetive. 2. This article is on the front page of the NSFP Fall 2025 Issue! (I mean talk about major accomplishments!)

REFLECTION

I'm incredibly greatful to have joined the paper as early as I did. I mean my first semester, of my first year of college, is such a crazy amazing time to join. I have wanted to be a journalist since forever, and I've always known that one of the most important steps to make it in the field is to have written for your college paper during your time there.

With that being said, this is only the very beginning of my college news reporter journey. I hope to use the next 4-6 years wisely, and make the most of the time and opportunities granted to me.
